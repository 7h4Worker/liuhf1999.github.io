## Publication Details

**Authors:** H. Liu, Z. Wang, R. Li, X. Zhao, T. Xu, T. Zhou, H. Hu

**Venue:** IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (accepted Oct 23, 2025)

**Citation:** H. Liu, Z. Wang, R. Li, X. Zhao, T. Xu, T. Zhou, H. Hu. (2025). "A Novel Binocular-Encoded SSVEP Framework for Efficient VR-Based Brain-Computer Interface." *IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics*, accepted Oct 23, 2025.

## Abstract

We introduce binocular phase-aligned SSVEP encoding that improves information transfer rate and visual comfort when operating VR-based BCIs. The approach maintains robustness under head movement and depth changes, supporting richer multi-target interaction inside immersive environments.

## Key Contributions

- Binocular encoding strategies that enhance throughput in VR-oriented BCIs
- Improved robustness under head movement and depth variations
- Enhanced visual comfort for extended VR-BCI sessions
- Support for multi-target interaction in immersive environments
