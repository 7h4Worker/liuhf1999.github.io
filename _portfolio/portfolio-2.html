--- title: "Collaborative AR-BCI Demo Platform" excerpt: "Immersive multi-user BCI showcase built on Unity and wearable EEG." collection: portfolio header: teaser: project_ar-bci.jpg ---

This Unity-driven AR/VR platform enables multiple participants to co-control robots and vehicles using SSVEP-based brain signals. The system synchronizes shared targets, renders depth-stable cues, and combines live cognitive state feedback with physical device telemetry.

  • Hardware: Wearable ear-EEG and forehead EEG sensors with low-latency wireless streaming.
  • Software: Unity scenes orchestrating collaborative tasks, car and robotic arm control, and AR overlays.
  • Impact: Demonstrations used in competition finals and lab outreach to communicate practical BCI applications.
